Getting Started

You'll need a valid learner permit, which you can obtain from the NDLS. You must be at least 17 years old and pass the theory test first. We can help guide you through the process and provide theory test preparation materials.
This varies depending on your experience and natural ability. On average, most students need between 15-25 hours of professional instruction. Our structured curriculum helps track your progress through Standard, Intermediate, and Advanced levels.
You can book individual lessons at €45 per hour, or save money with our packages. The 5-hour package saves you €25, and the 10-hour package saves you €70 while providing more comprehensive instruction.

Lessons & Instruction

Every lesson includes use of our modern dual-control vehicle, fuel, insurance, and instruction from a qualified RSA-approved instructor. We also provide progress tracking and theory test support materials.
All our lessons are 60 minutes long. This gives enough time for proper instruction, practice, and feedback without becoming overwhelming for new learners.
Yes! We have Ray Ryan, Lisa O'Brien, and David Kelly available. You can request a specific instructor based on availability, or we can match you with the instructor best suited to your learning style.
We understand life happens! Please give us at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule. This allows us to offer the slot to another student and avoid any cancellation fees.

Mock Tests

Our 90-minute mock test replicates the actual driving test experience. You'll complete the same maneuvers, follow similar routes, and receive detailed feedback on your performance. It includes a comprehensive fault assessment and recommendations for improvement.
We recommend booking a mock test 1-2 weeks before your actual driving test, after you've completed most of your lessons. This gives you time to work on any areas identified during the mock test.
Our mock test service costs €65 for the full 90-minute session, including detailed feedback and a written assessment of your performance.

Booking & Payment

You can book through our contact form, call us directly at +353 1 234 5678, or email info@silverfoxdrivingschool.ie. We'll work with you to find convenient times that fit your schedule.
We accept cash, bank transfer, and all major credit/debit cards. Payment can be made per lesson or in advance for packages. Package payments offer the best value with significant savings.
Yes! We understand everyone has different schedules. We offer lessons seven days a week, including evenings and weekends, subject to instructor availability.

Test Preparation

Yes, we provide theory test support materials and guidance as part of our lessons. While we don't conduct theory tests, we help you understand the rules of the road and prepare for both theory and practical elements.
We're proud of our 95% pass rate. This high success rate is due to our structured curriculum, experienced instructors, and comprehensive test preparation including our mock test service.
Yes, we provide pick-up and drop-off within the Dublin area as part of our service. This makes lessons more convenient and allows us to practice in different areas.
